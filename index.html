Murders in the United States

By Sarah DeVoe

The Number of Murders in Each State

Throughout the U.S., the number of murders in 2019 ranges from the less than 10 to well above 1,000. The state with the most murders was California, and the state with the least murders was Florida, according to the avilable data. However, it is important to recognize that three of the states, Illinois, Florida, and Alabama, only had limited data available for 2019. The actual number of murders for these states may be higher. When not including these states with limited data, Vermont had the least amount of murders.

Murder Weapons used in California

California had the most murders in the U.S. in 2019. The weapons used in these murders included firearms like handguns, rifles and shotguns, as well as knives, cutting instruments, and body parts like hands, fists and feet. Weapons that did not fit in any of these categories were identified as "other." The largest number of murders were done with a handgun, and the second largest number of murders were done with an unknown type of firearm.
